## 🏃‍♂️ 14-Week Marathon Training Plan (5 Days/Week from Week 5 onward)

**Goal Time:** 3:30 (4:58/km pace)  
**Race Day:** October 19, 2025  
**Structure:**  
- **Tue:** Easy / Medium  
- **Wed:** Medium or Easy  
- **Thu:** Tempo / Intervals  
- **Sat or Sun:** Long Run  
- **Fri or Sun:** 5th run (from Week 5 onward; optional before)  
- **Interval Rest Guidelines:**  
  - < 1 km reps → **2 min jog**  
  - 1–1.2 km reps → **2.5–3 min jog**

### ✅ Summary Notes:
- Added 5th run from Week 5 onward for extra aerobic volume.  
- Increased key interval sessions (Week 6 → 8 reps, Week 8 → 4 x 2 km).  
- Long runs in Weeks 6 & 10 now have extended MP blocks.  
- All tempo runs now include 2 km warm-up & cooldown, run slightly faster than MP (4:50–4:55/km).  
- MP segments in long runs remain locked to ~4:58/km pace.  
- Plan remains realistic but slightly more demanding — higher specificity for sub-3:30.

Formula for KMs needed for desired time:  20 + (3010 / (time (in min) - 100))
